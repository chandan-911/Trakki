Mission

The main target is to mentor students from Government Schools in Rural Punjab, helping them progress in life and pursue higher studies.

Empower

Trakki empowers students by providing them with the tools and support they need to succeed in their education and pursue higher studies.

Transform

Trakki aims to transform the lives of students by helping them realize their full potential and achieve their goals.

Inspire

Trakki inspires students to dream big and believe in themselves, showing them that they can overcome challenges and succeed.

Impact

Trakki creates a positive impact on society by supporting education and empowering students to build a better future for themselves and their communities.
